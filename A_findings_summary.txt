Insight: The revenue top products list and the volume top products list don't coincide. While the company uses high volume, low margin impulse products to attract customers, its profitability comes from premium wholesale products.

Question-4:

Insight: It is a pure wholesale business (B2B). Since over a quarter of our profit comes from a few select clients, we depend on maintaining positive relations with these important VIP clients.

Question-5:

The average global order size is currently set at £518.76 per invoice. Average domestic UK invoices are valued at £487.95, while international invoices that are not from the UK reach as high as £813.45.
Insight: The international customers order significantly less frequently because of the global shipping barrier but tend to make larger bulk orders during each purchase to reduce transport costs.
